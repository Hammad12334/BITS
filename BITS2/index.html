Anxiety, depression and other form of distress, effect hundreds of millions of people across the world. This is a huge problem in this day and age and people need to be constantly reminded of the detrimental effects of not taking care of your own mental health. We need to change the stigma of mental health and make sure everyone is aware and maintains a healthy mental state. This app brings our own mental and physical health into our consciousness. In modern society people are always busy and never take time off to think what they can do better, and improve productivity and have more time to do the things they enjoy or at least try to change their perception and reaction to life's stressors. Our moods and feelings are represented by our levels of stress (both eustress e.g. excitement and distress e.g. anxiety). Our levels of distress can decrease depending on the activities and goals we accomplished, as well as our perception of the distresses. We understand that as humans, we tend to want purpose for meaning in life, whilst balancing high ambition with a balanced lifestyle.
